LSAMP Center

The LSAMP center located in Daggy Hall 101 is fully equipped for everything from hardcore studying to brain-numbing relaxation. Active LSAMP-affiliated students can take advantage of large group study rooms and small two- or single-person study rooms. It’s a perfect place to sit down and eat lunch or dinner. The LSAMP center has a refrigerator and microwave; FREE coffee, tea, and hot cocoa; and printing and scanning.

Amenities

  • FREE coffee, hot cocoa, tea
  • FREE printing, scanning (blk/wht)
  • Access to microwave
  • Lounge area to relax
  • Study rooms (for individuals/groups)

Tutoring

The LSAMP Center offers free tutoring for the following subjects:

  • Biology
  • Chemical Engineering
  • Chemistry
  • English
  • Physics
  • Pre-Med
