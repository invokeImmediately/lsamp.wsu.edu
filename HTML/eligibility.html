Eligibility

Eligibility for the WSU-LSAMP program is restricted to underrepresented minority* students in any STEM-related field.

Additional Eligibility

  • Enrolled at WSU in an undergraduate STEM major.
  • Expressed interest in pursuing a STEM baccalaureate degree.
  • Expressed interest in pursuing an advanced degree in a STEM discipline.
  • US citizen or permanent resident.

Requirements

  • Completed LSAMP Application form on file.
  • Nomination from LSAMP campus coordinator.

*African American/Black, Hispanic, Native American/Indian, and Pacific Islander. Please review the NSF Program Solicitation for more information.
