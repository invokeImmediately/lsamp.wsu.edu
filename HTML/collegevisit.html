Who Are We?

PNW LSAMP and the Voiland College of Engineering and Architecture provide MESA Community College Programs, LSAMP CC Scholars, and other science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM) students with pathways to be successful in STEM fields at the university level. PNW LSAMP creates a culture and community of success for STEM majors. Through LSAMP, underrepresented minority students receive mentoring, tutoring, career guidance, professional development, and graduate school preparation. In addition, students have the opportunity to create their own social network through the different social events and activities facilitated by PNW LSAMP.
