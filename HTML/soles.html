SOLES Latino Parent Conference

Friday, February 15, 2019

You and one of your parents are invited to the annual Latino Parent Conference at Washington State University in Pullman, WA. Hosted by the Society of Latinx Engineers and Scientists (SOLES) and the Society of Hispanic Professional Engineers Junior (SHPE Jr.), this unique program is for Spanish-speaking parents of high school students who are interested in science, technology, engineering, or math (STEM), and the conference is conducted entirely in Spanish for the parents to better engage!

The conference includes:

  • Hands-on science and engineering workshops
  • Free access to laboratories and materials
  • Financial aid presentation
  • WSU campus tour
  • Meals (breakfast, lunch, and dinner)

Please note: transportation is not provided.

Sponsored by: Voiland College of Engineering and Architecture, and WSU SOLES.

Registration

Registration is taken on a first come, first served basis. We will only be accepting the first 100 families. Preference will be given to students of good academic standing, GEAR UP, MESA and/or upper-level classmen.

Late Registration Fee: $25 per person after December 24, 2018
Deadline: Monday, January 28, 2019
