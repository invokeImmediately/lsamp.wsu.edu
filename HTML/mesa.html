Connecting with Community Colleges Through MESA!

PNW LSAMP provides MESA Community College Program students with pathways to be successful in science, technology, engineering and mathematics (STEM) fields. It creates a culture and community of success for STEM majors. Through PNW LSAMP, underrepresented minority students receive tutoring, career guidance, professional development and graduate school preparation.

Recruitment and Orientation Events

June-July
Alive! New Student Orientation
Alive! is a two and a half day program offered during the summer months designed to prepare you for the start of classes in the fall.

August
Fall Alive!
Fall Alive! is a three-day orientation and registration program designed to prepare you for the start of classes in the fall.

October
Connecting Community Colleges to WSU
This one-day campus visit is for community college STEM students interested in transferring to WSU Pullman. Join us for an informative campus visit; learn about admissions, financial aid, and STEM-related resources.

October-November
LSAMP is on the Road! Each fall, LSAMP Staff visits community colleges across Washington to recruit underrepresented minorities students interested in STEM. Contact us for more information.

March
Transfer Alive!
Transfer Alive! is a two-day orientation and registration program designed to prepare admitted students for their transition to Washington State University.
