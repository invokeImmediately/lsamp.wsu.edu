Tutoring

The LSAMP Center offers FREE peer-to-peer tutoring for STEM (science, technology, engineering, and mathematics) majors. Our tutors are exceptional students who are dedicated to furthering their own academic goals and take pride in sincerely helping their peers.

The LSAMP Center offers tutoring for the following subjects

  • Math
  • Biology
  • Chemical Engineering
  • Chemistry
  • English
  • Physics
  • Pre-Med Classes
  • Computer Science
  • Electrical Engineering

Location: LSAMP tutoring is located in Daggy Hall 109.
